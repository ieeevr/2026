The IEEE Conference on Virtual Reality and 3D User Interfaces (IEEE VR) is the premier international event for the presentation of research results in the broad area of virtual, aumented, and mixed reality (VR/AR/MR).

IEEE VR brings together leaders, innovators, and influencers to disseminate the latest research and advancements being developed from around the world. Since 1993, IEEE VR has presented groundbreaking research and accomplishments by virtual reality pioneers: scientists, engineers, designers, and artists, paving the way for the future.

Our goal is for IEEE VR 2026 to provide the best experience for virtual attendees, following in the historical footsteps of the Virtual Experience of the Virtual Experience of VR 2025 in Saint-Malo.

Come join us for an unforgettable experience and explore the virtuality continuum in the premier event covering extended reality featuring oral presentations, posters, research demos, tutorials, and workshops.
